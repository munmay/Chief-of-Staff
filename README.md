# Chief of Staff

> This agent team runs on Google Apps Script and requires a Google account. All core data lives in Google Sheets. [Setup guide](https://www.notion.so/33202f9e5d568163af12f370c152dfff) • [Demo](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1O7wp5dwhsHkeymapBkf4MyMgbTGXfo9b9yRKzEZHKhY/edit?usp=sharing)

This is a team of agents, led by a Chief of Staff ([Feature list](https://www.notion.so/33202f9e5d568163af12f370c152dfff?pvs=25#37f33be6d7aa463ab4af52bbe53a57af)).

- You can communicate with your Chief of Staff via Slack*, Telegram*, and/or WhatsApp. You can also forward emails to them.
- You can send them your goals, thoughts, meeting notes, and docs. They can also gather context from Google Drive*, Notion*, Smartsheet*, and OneDrive*.
- They collects context from across your work, store it in one place (Google Sheets — source of truth). That’s where they work from.
- With all of these information, the Chief of Staff and their team propose work, flag drift, and generate updates on Google Sheets — based on your goals and north star.
  - Optionally it can create tasks on SmartSheet — approve, update, assign, reassign, add notes, set due dates (and send reminders).
- You can assign work to them, and they will create/update Google Docs.

## Operating Principles

- Shared memory first: everything is recorded into the Google Sheet context store. You can see exactly what the team did and why.
- North Star alignment: the `🎯 Company Profile` gives the team mission, goals, anti-goals, and principles so it can filter distractions.
- Goals over signals — every task gets tested before it reaches you.

## Meet the team

[How they operate](https://www.notion.so/33202f9e5d56819ebc37ebe6210452eb) • [Office hours](https://www.notion.so/33202f9e5d56819ebc37ebe6210452eb?pvs=25#21c0f50cd2bc409e989211df724c80a1)

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Intake Lead | Pulls in signals from every connected tool and writes them into the shared notebook. Nothing gets missed. |
| Planning Lead | Reads the notebook, names the situation, and proposes only the tasks worth doing — after testing them first. |
| Delivery Lead | Watches open work for signs of stalling. Flags anything that hasn't moved in over a week. |
| Briefing Lead | Writes a short, sharp update — bottom line first — every Friday. |
| Research Analyst | Monitors outside sources you choose and feeds relevant learnings back to the team. |
| Editorial Director | Reviews team output for clarity and quality before it reaches you. |
| Knowledge Manager | Keeps the shared notebook clean, organized, and trustworthy over time. |
| Program Manager | Watches the task queue for stuck work, slow flow, and hidden blockers. |
